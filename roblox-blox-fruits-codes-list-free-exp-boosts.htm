Blox Fruits codes [February 2023]: Free EXP boosts and Beli

Redeem these Blox Fruits codes for free EXP boosts and Beli

Guide by Ollie Toms Guides Editor
Additional contributions by
  • Rebecca Jones
Updated on Feb. 21, 2023

Looking for the latest Blox Fruits codes? Blox Fruits is a Roblox experience dedicated to proving your mum right: eat plenty of fresh fruit and you'll develop superpowers. (Everyone's mum used to tell them that, right?) Like many games on Roblox, Blox Fruits is all about accruing power quickly and efficiently for your character to overcome opponents, which you do primarily by eating different kinds of fruit. There are also a lot of swords, which thankfully you don't have to eat, but which do look pretty cool.

Blox Fruits codes are a free and easy way to get bonuses and boosts. Most of the codes give you free character EXP by granting you a certain amount of time during which the XP you gain is doubled. They can also reward you with the occasional currency injection or cosmetic item. Read on for a list of all working Blox Fruits codes and instructions on how to redeem them.

Feel free to redeem these codes all at once, but know that the effect doesn't stack: only the duration stacks. So if you use two codes at once that both give you 20 minutes of 2x EXP, then you won't get 20 minutes of 4x EXP. Instead you'll get 40 minutes of 2x EXP.

How to redeem Blox Fruits codes

It's very simple to redeem Blox Fruits codes. First, open up Blox Fruits. Then click on the little Twitter icon (the blue bird) on the left-hand side, next to the volume and options buttons.

Clicking on this Twitter icon will make a box appear where you can type in your codes one by one. Just click "Try" after typing in each code to see if it works.
